About

I'm a Bachelor's student in Mathematics at the Università degli Studi di Napoli Federico II, concurrently enrolled in the Advanced Programme in Mathematics, Physics and Engineering at the Scuola Superiore Meridionale. My thesis work looks at category theory and topos theory — from the classical notions of categories and functors to naturality, universality, and elementary topoi.

Born in 2004 in Giugliano in Campania, near Naples. Outside of coursework I play piano and guitar, and run regularly.
